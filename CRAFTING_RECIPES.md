# DNA SMP Crafting Recipes

## DNA Upgrade (Less Expensive)

**Craftable infinite times**

### Recipe:
```
L L L
L D L
L L L
```
- **L** = Lapis Lazuli (8x)
- **D** = Diamond (1x)

### Result:
- **DNA Upgrade Item**
- **Effect**: +1 DNA Level
- **Description**: A simple way to boost your DNA level with common materials

---

## Premium DNA Trade (Very Expensive)

**Craftable infinite times**

### Recipe:
```
D N E
N S N
D N E
```
- **D** = Dragon Egg (1x) + Dragon Head (2x)
- **N** = Netherite Ingot (4x)
- **E** = Dragon Head (2x)
- **S** = Nether Star (1x)

### Full Materials Required:
- 1x Dragon Egg
- 4x Netherite Ingots
- 2x Dragon Heads
- 1x Nether Star

### Result:
- **Premium DNA Trade Item**
- **Effect**: +2 DNA Level + Dragon Breath Ability Unlock
- **Description**: The ultimate DNA upgrade with expensive Nether materials

---

## DNA Trader NPC

A special NPC trader that sells exclusive DNA items:

1. **DNA Upgrade** (4 Emeralds)
2. **Premium DNA Trade** (16 Emeralds)

Both can be purchased **unlimited times**.

---

## Item Icons

Both items feature **DNA Helix designs**:

### DNA Upgrade Icon
- Blue/Cyan DNA Helix (Lapis Lazuli theme)
- Represents basic DNA enhancement

### Premium DNA Trade Icon
- Dark Purple/Netherite DNA Helix
- Represents advanced DNA enhancement
- Dragon effects around the helix

---

## How to Use

1. **Gather Materials**: Collect the required ingredients
2. **Craft Item**: Use the crafting table
3. **Consume**: Right-click or use the DNA item
4. **Gain DNA**: Your DNA level increases immediately
5. **Repeat**: Both recipes are craftable infinite times

## Tips

- DNA Upgrade is great for early game progression
- Premium Trade is for late-game players with access to Nether materials
- Dragon Egg can be obtained by defeating the Ender Dragon
- Nether Stars drop from Withers
- Save expensive materials for the Premium Trade if you want the Dragon Breath ability!
