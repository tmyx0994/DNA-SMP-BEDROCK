# DNA SMP Weapon System Guide

## Weapon Assignment by Mob Type

Each mob gets a unique weapon when they join:

| Mob | Weapon | Enchantments | Special |
|-----|--------|--------------|---------|
| **Creeper** | Diamond Pickaxe | Efficiency V, Unbreaking III | Mining focused |
| **Enderman** | Diamond Axe | Sharpness V, Knockback II | Teleport damage |
| **Spider** | Diamond Sword | Sharpness V, Fire Aspect II | Climbing agility |
| **Blaze** | Diamond Pickaxe | Efficiency V, Unbreaking III | Fire resistance |
| **Ghast** | Diamond Axe | Sharpness V, Knockback II | Flight based |
| **Wither Skeleton** | Bow ⭐ | Power V, Punch II, Infinity | **EXCLUSIVE - Only this mob has bow!** |
| **Ender Dragon** | Diamond Sword | Sharpness V, Fire Aspect II | Ultimate weapon |

---

## Weapon Details

### Pickaxe (Creeper & Blaze)
- **Enchantments**:
  - Efficiency V - Mine blocks faster
  - Unbreaking III - Lasts longer
- **Best for**: Rapid mining and block breaking
- **Combat**: Good for building fortifications quickly

### Axe (Enderman & Ghast)
- **Enchantments**:
  - Sharpness V - More damage
  - Knockback II - Knock enemies back
- **Best for**: Close combat with knockback
- **Combat**: Push enemies away, control space

### Sword (Spider & Ender Dragon)
- **Enchantments**:
  - Sharpness V - Maximum damage
  - Fire Aspect II - Set enemies on fire
- **Best for**: Direct combat, dealing damage
- **Combat**: Most damaging melee weapon

### Bow (Wither Skeleton Only) ⭐
- **Enchantments**:
  - Power V - Maximum arrow damage
  - Punch II - Knockback on arrows
  - Infinity - Unlimited arrows (no durability)
- **Bonus**: Start with 64 arrows
- **Best for**: Long-range combat from distance
- **Combat**: Snipe enemies, unique ranged advantage

## How Weapons Work in Game

### Getting Your Weapon

When you join the world:
1. Random mob assigned (1-7)
2. Corresponding weapon given automatically
3. Weapon appears in inventory
4. Weapon has all enchantments ready

### Weapon Strategy

**Melee Players (Pickaxe/Axe/Sword)**:
- Close range combat
- Need to engage enemies directly
- Better for 1v1 fights
- Can mine/gather resources

**Ranged Player (Bow)**:
- Long range attacks
- Can snipe from distance
- More arrows = more shots
- Stay away from close combat

## Weapon Duels Guide

### Pickaxe vs Other Weapons
- Fair against Sword (sword wins)
- Good against Bow (hard to hit)
- Fair against Axe

### Axe vs Other Weapons
- Fair against Sword (roughly equal)
- Good knockback advantage
- Can knock Bow users off cliffs

### Sword vs Other Weapons
- Wins most 1v1s (highest damage)
- Fast attack speed
- Best pure damage dealer

### Bow vs Other Weapons
- Wins from distance
- Loses in close range
- Needs height advantage
- Can kite enemies

## Strategic Gameplay

### Early Game (DNA 0-2)
- Use your weapon to gather resources
- Mine for materials
- Focus on building defenses

### Mid Game (DNA 2-4)
- Engage in weapon-based combat
- Use environment (cliffs, water)
- Pickaxe: Build towers
- Axe: Use knockback tactically
- Sword: Aggressive close combat
- Bow: Snipe from distance

### Late Game (DNA 4-5)
- Combine weapon + ability
- Sword users get Dragon Breath (powerful!)
- Bow users stay ranged with Wither Skeleton ability
- Pickaxe/Axe users use abilities for damage

## Weapon Progression

As you level up, consider:
1. **Get better armor** first
2. **Collect materials** for fortifications
3. **Use weapon enchantments** effectively
4. **Combine with abilities** when you reach 5 DNA

## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch weapons?**
A: No, you're locked to your mob's weapon for balance

**Q: Why does Wither Skeleton have a bow?**
A: For unique ranged gameplay with limited other ranged options

**Q: Do weapons break?**
A: No, they have Unbreaking enchantments (mostly)

**Q: Can I use my ability without a weapon?**
A: Yes, abilities work independent of weapons

**Q: Which weapon is best?**
A: Sword for damage, Bow for control, Pickaxe for utility

**Q: Can I get more weapons?**
A: Only your assigned weapon. No duplication.
